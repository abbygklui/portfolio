Observational Grid

Typographic Layouts

Duration : 3 Weeks

Type : Personal Project

Responsibilities : Layout Design, Typography

Tools : Indesign, Illustration

About this Project


The goal for this project was to create typographic layouts focusing on the composition and structure. First, I found several articles with dynamic images. I then converted images into black and white and analyzed the image to integrate typography. The objective is to use a grid based on the nature of the image to form several typographic compositions that take all details, rules of typography, as well as content into consideration. ​
The two photos and content I chose to use for this project are from Tonkin's Matte Swim Campaign and Nyakim Gatwech's empowering interview: ​
​

Phoebe Tonkin's Campaign
Nyakim Gatwech's Interview

​

The Process


First, I selected two photos and analyze it in terms of its content, structure, form and white space relationships. I used grid rulers to form lines based on the nature of the photographic image. I then created many typographic iterations of my layout. I then selected 8 works that conveyed what I wished to achieve. Each composition pays attention the direction and movement on the image.

My Reflection


Through this assignment, I became more confident in my basic typographic skills, such as using micro and macro typographic elements. Working with different grids allowed me to experiment with forms and spacings while paying attention to typographic hierarchy. I found it challenging to choose a final layout because I wanted to continue improving and creating more compositions. Overall, working on this project was very enjoyable and I am satisfied with my final layout.
