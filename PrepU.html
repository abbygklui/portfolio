PREPU

Duration : 26 Weeks (To Present)
Tools: Miro, Figma
Strategies : UX/UI Design, Front-End Development
Team : Abby Lui, Carol Chen, Mengchan Zhao, Moneet Tiwana, Anito So

INTRODUCTION

PrepU is a web app that satisfies the market for an accessible and effective job interview preparation program to help develop compelling communication skills and advance career goals. This project was made possible with the help of the ADA Mentorship program, ran with Women in Tech Society (WITS) to help students develop a technical project with the help of professional developers.


THE INTERVIEW


Customize features, view statistics, and provide feedback on the homepage. To start your interview, set your settings and view your assessment on the side as your present.




THE INSIGHTS

The user will be shown a detailed summary of feedback based on the interview. They will also see the level of enthusiasm, number of filler words, number of repeated words, and pause time, and when exactly it happened during the interview. On the performance page, the user could compare view their past interview feedback.
