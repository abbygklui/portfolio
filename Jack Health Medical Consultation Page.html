Jack Health Medical Consultation Redesign

Telehealth Design

Duration : 2 Weeks

Role : Sole Designer

Responsibilities : User Research, Marketing Design, UX/UI, User Testing

Tools : Figma, Adobe Illustrator

About


Jack Health is a men's healthcare service that offers prescription based lifestyle medications through virtual visits with licensed Canadian doctors and practitioners. In January 2021, I collaborated with two designers to completely redesign all pages of Jack & Jill Health. This case study will focus on the the redesign of the medical consultation page, which is one of the pages I worked on individually.

Problem

The current medical consultation page is outdated and affects customer engagement and credibility.

Similar to the other pages of the current Jack website, the design neglected arranging an information architecture and following values that would strengthen trust with our target market.

Old Medical Consultation Page

How Jack Health Works


Users can complete a free online consultation through the medical consultation page for a product to be prescribed by a doctor based on their medical history. The user can also first select the treatment if they are already sure which medication they want, then complete their consultation after. Once the prescription is approved and fufilled by Jack Health’s pharmacy, the medication will be shipped to their door.

""How might I effectively create a medical consultion page that will help strengthen trust and engagement with Jack Health’s users?"

My Approach


  1. Gather insights based on this problem

  2. Research and Analyze Competitors

  3. Explore solutions that resolve my objective

  4. Iterate and design a final prototype

Problem Validation


To find insight outside of my personal experience, I looked through customer service and forums. I noticed that a common problem was the fact that customers were unsure if Jack Health was reputable.

I then interviewed four first time users to test out the site and interact with this page. Here is some of their comments:

The design of this page feels and looks old, so It doesn’t look trustworthy.

The colours and button placements are throwing me off, it makes it hard to navigate and continue with a consult.

It would be nice if there was more information here so I can confirm that I want to do a consultation.

Key Takeaways

  • The overall layout and design of the page influences the level of trust and engagement
  • Additional content about Jack Health can help the user determine if this is relevent what they want.

The Objectives


Based on my research, I my defined two key objectives

Improve Information Architecture

Presents information and content to build trust from the perspective of a user


Redesign Interface and Design Structure

Refine the page to match the new branding used in the redesign of Jack Health and to be be easlier to navigate

Objective 1 : Improve Information Architecture

Competitive Analysis


I worked alongside the growth lead, Bucky, to analyze the homepages of direct and adjacent competitors of Jack Health. We wanted to understand different sections that are valuable to the user. Viewing their homepage rather then their consultation page helps us recognize which major sections on the websites

Key Takeaways

  • Not all websites offer free consultations
  • Telehealth websites commonly include these sections:
    • How it Works
    • Social Proof
    • Reviews

Testing

I reached out to a designer and the growth lead for feedback and insight on my current design

Key Takeaways

  • Inform users about transparent pricing
  • Add the Trustpilot logo to connect each review

Objective 2 : Redesign Interface and Design Structure

Jack Health Branding


Jack Health’s new identity is minimal and fun. It uses more product and portrait images with positive, pointed messaging. Colours and lighter and use more earthly tones, while the packaging is bright and cheerful.


I used the packaging mockups to create a colourful and fun element and stay consistent to the other pages. I created a few iterations on how I wanted to lay out the buttons.

Hierarchy

Text on the left allows the header to be larger and more comprehensive. The fact that medical consults are completely free should be emphasized.


Contrast

This dark blue and light beige hold higher contrast compared to the colour combinations of the earlier button iterations

Final


The month following the launch of the free consultation page became the most successful one yet - with a surge in sales and an improvement in customer ratings. This project helped me learn a lot about how to determine features that would be valuable to the user prior to completing a digital consultation. I found myself constantly thinking about content and placement that would effectively communicate Jack Health’s values and products. It was extremely interesting to focus on the importance of design in business.
